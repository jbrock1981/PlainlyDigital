This privacy policy applies to the Plainly Digital LLC corporate website at
**plainlydigital.com**. Each app in the suite (ClearDoc, SitterSheet, Plainly,
Vinla, Accomplishly, AI Life Advisor) has its own privacy policy linked
from the app and from the app's page on this site.

## What we collect on this site

Almost nothing. The plainlydigital.com marketing site is a static informational
website. It contains no contact forms, no account system, no tracking pixels,
no third-party analytics scripts, no advertising trackers, and no chat
widgets.

We deliberately collect the minimum technical data needed to serve the site:

- **Server logs**: when you visit a page, our hosting provider (Cloudflare
  Pages) records standard request metadata — your IP address, the page
  requested, the timestamp, and your browser's user-agent string. These logs
  are kept for a short period for operational and security purposes (DDoS
  detection, error troubleshooting) and are not used to build profiles of
  visitors.
- **Cookies**: this site sets no cookies. We do not use Google Analytics,
  Hotjar, Meta Pixel, or any other third-party tracking. If we ever add
  analytics, it will be a privacy-preserving, cookieless tool such as
  Cloudflare Web Analytics, and this policy will be updated.

The apps in the Plainly Digital suite collect different categories of data

- [ClearDoc privacy policy](/cleardoc/privacy)

## How we use any information

We only use the limited request metadata above to operate, secure, and
troubleshoot the site. We do not sell, share, or rent visitor data to third
parties.

## Your rights

If you live in the EU/EEA (GDPR), the United Kingdom (UK GDPR), California
(CCPA/CPRA), or any other jurisdiction with similar laws, you may have the
right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data
we hold about you. Because we don't maintain a database of visitor identities
on this site, the only data we'd be able to surface is the limited server
logs above, scoped to your IP address.

To exercise any right, email
[apps@plainlydigital.com](mailto:apps@plainlydigital.com). We will respond
within 30 days.

## Children

This site is not directed at children under 13. Apps that may be used by
younger users disclose age requirements in their own privacy policies and
follow COPPA where applicable.

We will update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be
