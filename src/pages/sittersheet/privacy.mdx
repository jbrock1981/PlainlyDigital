SitterSheet is a mobile app published by Plainly Digital LLC ("we," "us,"
"our") that turns your wizard answers into a comprehensive pet/house-sitter
guide and lets you share it with a 30-day expiring link. SitterSheet handles
**sensitive household and pet information**, so this policy is detailed.
Please read it.

## What SitterSheet collects

When you complete the wizard, SitterSheet collects:

- **Pet information**: name, species, age, feeding schedule, medications,
  exercise needs, behavior quirks, and emergency vet contact information.
- **House information**: address, WiFi network name and password, alarm
  code, trash and recycling schedule, neighbor or backup-contact name and
  phone number.
- **Owner contact information**: your phone number and how to reach you in
  an emergency.
- **Account data**: a Firebase Authentication user ID. SitterSheet supports
  anonymous accounts and email-and-password accounts.
- **Usage data**: counts of guides created, subscription/unlock state, rate
  limit counters used for abuse prevention.

## How sensitive fields are stored

This is the part most people care about, so we'll be specific:

- **WiFi password and alarm code** are stored on your device using
  the operating system's secure storage — **iOS Keychain** or **Android
  Keystore**, via [`expo-secure-store`](https://docs.expo.dev/versions/latest/sdk/securestore/).
  These fields are not stored in plaintext on the device. If your device
  does not support secure storage (for example, the web preview), SitterSheet
  will warn you and refuse to persist sensitive draft data — your wizard
  progress for those fields will not be saved between sessions.
- **Other guide content** (pet info, addresses, vet contact, etc.) is sent
  to our servers and stored in **Google Firebase Firestore**, encrypted in
  transit and at rest by Google. Sensitive fields included in the final
  generated guide are also stored alongside the rest of the guide so the
  guide can be rendered when shared.

## What SitterSheet sends to third parties

To provide the service, SitterSheet shares limited data with three providers:

- **Anthropic** — when you generate a guide, your wizard answers are sent
  to Anthropic's Claude API to format them into a friendly markdown guide.
  Per Anthropic's
  [Commercial Terms of Service](https://www.anthropic.com/legal/commercial-terms),
  Anthropic does not retain API inputs or outputs for training.
- **Google Firebase** — Authentication, Firestore, Cloud Functions, and
  Hosting (for share pages) are operated by Google.
- **RevenueCat** — handles your `$4.99` lifetime unlock purchase, in
  accordance with their [privacy policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy).
  RevenueCat receives a hashed user ID and the product purchased.

## Sharing — read this carefully

When you tap **Share**, SitterSheet generates a unique 32-character (128-bit)
random token and creates a public web page at
`plainlydigital.com/g/<token>` that renders your guide in plain HTML.

**Anyone who has that link can view the guide.** The link is hard to
guess — 128 bits of entropy — but it is not access-controlled. Treat the
link as you would a key to your home, because if your guide includes a
WiFi password and an alarm code, that's effectively what it is.

We mitigate the risk in three ways:

- Share links **expire automatically after 30 days**, after which the URL
  returns "410 Gone."
- You can **rotate** the share link from inside the app at any time, which
  permanently invalidates the previous link.
- Share pages are served with `X-Robots-Tag: noindex,nofollow` so search
  engines should not index them.

If you accidentally share a link with the wrong person, **rotate the link
immediately** in the app. Treat that as a security incident: if the wrong
person has your alarm code, change the code.

## Account deletion

You can delete your account at any time from **Settings → Delete account**.
Deletion permanently removes your Firebase Auth record and all stored
guides within 30 days. Active share links are immediately invalidated.
On-device secure-storage entries are deleted from your device when you
uninstall the app or call **Settings → Reset device data**.

You may also email [apps@plainlydigital.com](mailto:apps@plainlydigital.com)
to request deletion if the in-app flow does not work for you.

## AI disclosure

SitterSheet uses the Claude AI model from Anthropic to format your wizard
answers into a guide. AI is fallible. SitterSheet may rephrase, summarize,
or omit information you entered. **Always read the generated guide
carefully and edit anything that's wrong before sharing it with a sitter
who will rely on it.** SitterSheet is not a substitute for veterinary or
legal advice.

## Anonymous-account orphan-data risk

If you use SitterSheet anonymously and then uninstall the app or sign out
without first deleting your account, the anonymous user ID becomes
unreachable. Your guides remain in our database, encrypted at rest, but you
will not be able to log back in to recover or delete them. We retain
unreachable anonymous data for 12 months and then purge it on a rolling
schedule. Sign in with email if you want recovery.
